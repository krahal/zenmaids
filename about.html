WHO WE ARE

Zen Maids is not your typical home cleaning company. We go beyond cleaning, and aim to bring zen and peace into your home.

Our continuous pursuit for perfection has resulted in extremely satisfied and recurrent customers. Our focus is to really listen to our clients, understand their needs and provide the exceptional level of maid service that makes them relax with the confidence that the job will be done well. Our cleaning services are thorough, consistent and customized.

OUR MISSION IS TO:

  • Use environmentally friendly cleaning products
  • Provide stable jobs with resonable wages
  • Make you an extremely satisfied customer!
